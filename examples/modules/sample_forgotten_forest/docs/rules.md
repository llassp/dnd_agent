# Core Rules Reference

## Movement

- Difficult terrain costs double movement.
- You can move through a hostile creature's space if it is at least two sizes larger or smaller than you.
- Standing up from prone costs half your movement.

## Combat

### Attack Roll
d20 + Proficiency Bonus + relevant modifier. Must meet or exceed the target's Armor Class.

### Damage Roll
Roll the weapon's damage dice and add the relevant modifier. Critical hits allow additional dice to be rolled.

### Opportunity Attacks
When a creature moves out of your reach, you can make one attack as a reaction. Certain abilities like the Mobile feat can prevent this.

### Dodge
When you take the Dodge action, attacks against you have disadvantage until the start of your next turn.

## Resting

### Short Rest
At least 1 hour of light activity. You can spend Hit Dice to recover HP.

### Long Rest
8 hours of sleep with at least 6 hours of rest. You recover all HP, spend half your Hit Dice, and recover abilities.

## Ability Checks

### Advantage
Roll two d20s and take the higher result.

### Disadvantage
Roll two d20s and take the lower result.

### Passive Checks
Passive score = 10 + modifiers. Used when circumstances mean a result is automatic.

## Death Saving Throws

When reduced to 0 HP, roll d20 each turn:
- 10+: Success
- <10: Failure
- Natural 20: Regain 1 HP
- Natural 1: Two failures

Three successes stabilize. Three failures mean death.
