# The Forgotten Forest

## Overview

The Forgotten Forest is an ancient woodland shrouded in mystery and magical enchantments. Located northeast of the village of Millbrook, this forest has been cut off from civilization for over a century.

## Notable Locations

### The Whispering Glade
A central clearing where the Feywild touches the Material Plane. The air here sparkles with magical energy, and travelers report hearing whispers in languages they don't recognize.

### The Twisted Oak
A massive oak tree at the forest's heart, estimated to be over 1,000 years old. Its trunk is wide enough to shelter a small party, and its roots extend throughout the forest.

### The Ruined Shrine
What remains of an old temple dedicated to a forgotten deity. Adventurers who rest here overnight sometimes receive visions or guidance.

## Key NPCs

### Old Magnus (Hermit)
A elderly human who has lived in the forest for decades. He knows the safe paths and can provide guidance, but his mind is not entirely reliable.

### The Forest Spirit (Unknown Entity)
Appears as a swirling mass of leaves and light. It guards the forest and tests those who enter with riddles and challenges.

## Adventure Hooks

1. The village of Millbrook has lost contact with their logging expedition.
2. Strange lights have been seen near the forest's edge.
3. A merchant seeks rare herbs that grow only within the Twisted Oak's roots.
